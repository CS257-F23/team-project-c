About PySpill

Introducing PySpill, an app designed for tracking pipeline spills across the nation. Users can access essential data like financial implications, environmental impact, and human injuries resulting from these incidents. By shedding light on the true costs and consequences of spills, we want to encourage a broader understanding of the pipeline industry.

Features

  • Many pages feature a map! Click on a point to learn more about the particular spill
  • Lookup all oil spills caused by a particular company
  • Lookup all oil spills that have occurred at a particular location
  • A leaderboard featuring the top ten worst offending companies by total volume of oil released
  • A map of all oil spills in the United States overlayed on top of a map of oil pipelines
