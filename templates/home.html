PySpill


Welcome to PySpill, a web tool made for viewing pipeline spills across the U.S.

We have two features:

  • Lookup by company: lookup data on pipeline spills from a company. Use the following url /lookup/company/<company_name>
    (example 1): To look at the company CONOCOPHILLIPS, try /lookup/company/CONOCOPHILLIPS
    (example 2): To look at the company LOOP INC, try /lookup/company/loop%20inc

  • Lookup by location: lookup data on pipeline spill in a location. Use the following url /lookup/location/<state>/<county>/<city>. County and city are both optional inputs, just use a blank space in the URL to leave them blank
    (example 1): To look at spills in the state of Texas, try /lookup/location/TX
    (example 2): To look up spill in Orange County, CA try /lookup/location/MN/rice
    (example 3): To look up spill in Faribault, MN try /lookup/location/MN/rice/Faribault or /lookup/location/MN/%20/Faribault
